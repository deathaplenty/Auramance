Very small squirrel humanoids. They're small (about 40 centimeters tall, or 1/4 the size of the average human) and fast, and some are even capable of speech.

## Available Magic
- Consultation

## Modifiers
- Health = 5

- +10 movement
- Athletics +3 when used for climbing
- Evasion +5
