## Summary
  A simple spell that will generically harm the target in some way. Due to its simple nature, it is easy to learn and cheap to cast.
## Requirement
  Auramancy 2
## Effect
  1d4 Magic Damage
## Cast Time
  2 actions
## Range
  30 meters
